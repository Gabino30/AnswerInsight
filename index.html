How it works

Three steps from your materials to a focused, role-specific brief.

1

Send your materials

Resume, job posting, target company, and interview notes.

We start with what you already have — no lengthy new forms to fill out. Share your resume, the job posting, the company you're interviewing with, and any notes from earlier rounds. The more context you give us, the sharper and more specific your brief will be.
2

Get your brief

Opening pitch, likely questions, STAR stories, technical prep, and weak spots.

You'll receive a ready-to-use opening pitch in your own voice, the questions most likely to come up for this specific role, a bank of STAR stories built from your real experience, technical and role-specific talking points, and an honest look at the weak spots worth tightening before you walk in.
3

Prep with focus

Use the 24-hour prep sheet before the interview.

In the final day before your interview, use the condensed prep sheet — a one-page summary of your pitch, your top stories, and the questions to expect — so you walk in confident instead of re-reading a ten-page document the morning of.
